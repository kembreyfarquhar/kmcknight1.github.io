About Me

Katie McKnight - Full stack web developer in the making

A quick intro

I was first introduced to the world of code by a very dear friend of mine. He was attending Lambda School, something I'd never heard of, and was quick to tell me how amazing it had been thus far. After a few months of him reiterating how truly INCREDIBLE it was and how much he was learning and growing through it... I was finally convinced to pick up my laptop and attempt my first line of code. A very fast and basic few days of HTML and CSS flew by, and before I knew it I was deep-diving straight into JavaScript. From there I spent all day, everyday learning as much as I could about code. I was sucked in... no hope for me to ever get out. Now immersed in the world of code, I have been working on various projects and hope to, someday soon, work for an amazing company.
